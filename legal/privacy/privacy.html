Privacy Policy

Last modified: 9/22/2024

This Privacy Policy outlines the practices of Shoot the Breeze and its sub-brands—Shoot the Breeze Studio, Dream Crafters, and Aether's Mind—(collectively referred to as “we”, “us”, or “our”) regarding the collection, use, and protection of your personal information when you interact with our websites and services. By accessing or using our services, you consent to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy.

I. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data. We are committed to maintaining the privacy and security of your information and encourage you to review this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

II. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: This includes data you provide directly, such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details when you register or use our services.
  • Usage Data: Information automatically collected about your interactions with our websites, including your IP address, browser type, the pages you visit, and the time spent on those pages.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor activity on our services and store certain information, enhancing your user experience.

III. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used for various purposes, including:

  • To Provide and Maintain Our Services: Ensuring the functionality and security of our websites and services.
  • To Improve Our Services: Analyzing usage data to enhance user experience and service delivery.
  • To Communicate with You: Sending you updates, newsletters, marketing communications, and responding to your inquiries.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: Adhering to applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
  • To Protect Our Rights: Safeguarding the rights, property, and safety of our company, users, and others.
  • For Aether's Mind: If your startup idea is evaluated as feasible and beneficial, we may create the startup and offer you a 50% partnership and shares, necessitating the collection of additional personal information relevant to your business.
  • For Dream Crafters: Collecting data on your PC building preferences and project specifications to tailor our services and provide personalized recommendations.

IV. Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information in the following situations:

  • With trusted service providers who assist us in delivering our services and who are obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your information.
  • With business partners for joint services, promotions, or marketing activities.
  • When required by law or to respond to legal requests.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Shoot the Breeze, our users, or others.
  • In the case of Aether's Mind, we may share your information with potential investors or partners who are involved in the evaluation of your startup idea, under strict confidentiality agreements.

V. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request to correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal data under specific circumstances.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can withdraw your consent for data processing at any time, where applicable.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

VI. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

VII. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be effective when posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant changes through our services. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about our practices.

VIII. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at: shoot.the.breeze.studio.official@gmail.com.

I. Overview

Dream Crafters is committed to providing a personalized PC building experience. To do this effectively, we collect certain information relevant to your preferences and project requirements.

II. PC Building Data

We collect data on your PC specifications, preferences, and goals to ensure we provide the best custom building service. This includes the type of system, components, and any special features or customizations you request.

III. Data Protection

Your data is securely stored and used solely for the purpose of delivering the PC building services you request. We do not share this data with third parties unless you provide explicit consent.

I. Introduction

Aether's Mind is designed to help startups grow and succeed. As part of this process, we collect and evaluate information related to your startup ideas.

II. Startup Data Collection

When you submit your startup ideas to Aether's Mind, we collect information about your business concept, target market, potential customers, and financial projections. This data is critical for evaluating the feasibility and potential success of your idea.

III. Evaluation Process

Your startup idea will be evaluated by a team of experts, who will analyze the data you provide to determine its potential. If the idea is viable, we may offer to form a startup with you and share in the equity, giving you 50% ownership of the business.

IV. Confidentiality Agreement

All information related to your startup is kept strictly confidential. We use this information solely for the purpose of evaluating your business idea, and we will not share it with any third parties without your consent.
